Ribs

Description

Don't wanna go outside and grill but still want some ribs? Well, I got the perfect recipe for you! This is an easy recipe for some baby back ribs you can make in your oven. All you will need is a few ingrediants and a bit of free time to get these done.

Ingrediants

  • 2 racks of baby back pork ribs
  • 1/4 cup spicy brown mustard
  • 1 tbsp sea salt
  • 1 tbsp cracked black pepper
  • 1 1/2 cups barbecue sauce

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 275 degrees F.
  2. Rinse and pat the dry the ribs with a paper towel. Remove any silver skin or membrane from the ribs.
  3. Split the racks in half. This makes them easier to handle and gives you four good portions.
  4. Rub mustard into the top of the ribs.
  5. Season with sea salt and cracked black pepper.
  6. Wrap the ribs in foil, sealing the edges of the foil, making sealed packets.
  7. Place the ribs in the 275 degree preheated oven on a rack in the center of the oven. Cook the ribs for 2Â½ hours.
  8. Cut the foil packets open using kitchen shears and fold back the foil to expose the ribs. Liberally coat the ribs with barbecue sauce.
  9. Raise the oven temperature to 400 degrees F, you don't have to wait for it to preheat. Place the ribs back in the oven for 25 minutes.
  10. Set the oven to broil. When hot, place the baking tray under the broiler for 3-5 minutes. Repeat with an additional layer of bbq sauce, if desired. Serve the ribs.
